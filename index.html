jQuery Video Background plugin

Example of the jQuery Video Background plugin. Will place a resizable video in to the background of the page or designated element. Browsers that don't support the HTML5 video element will get an image if a poster image was designated.

Plugin parameters include:

  • webm:
  • ogv:
  • mp4:
  • poster:
  • autoplay:
  • preload:
  • loop:
  • controls:
  • controlsText:
  • preloader:
  • preloadHtml:
  • loadedCallback:

Videos from http://html5demos.com/.
